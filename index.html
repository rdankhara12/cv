Get ready to indulge in the best and most famous Indian street food - Vadapav, Samosa and Panipuri! Vadapav, also known as the 'Indian/Bombay burger,' is a delicious snack consisting of a deep-fried potato patty, sandwiched between a soft bread roll, and served with a huose made chef speciality tangy garlic chutney. It's the ultimate comfort food that satisfies your hunger pangs and leaves you feeling fulfilled.

Savor the flavors of India with Samosas - crispy triangular pastries filled with spiced potatoes and peas, and sometimes meat. A popular street food snack, Samosas are perfect for any occasion, whether you're in the mood for a quick snack or a full meal. These savory pastries are a cultural icon, reflecting the diverse and vibrant traditions of India. So, take a bite and experience the authentic taste of India with Samosas!

And, if you're looking for a burst of flavor in every bite, then try our Panipuri - the king of street food. It's a popular snack that consists of crispy hollow puri shells, filled with a spicy mixture of mashed potatoes, chickpeas, tamarind chutney, and a tangy mint-coriander water. Each bite is an explosion of flavors that will leave you wanting more.

At our restaurant, we take pride in serving the best and most authentic Vadapav and Panipuri, made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. So, come and enjoy the best of Indian street food with us, and experience the true essence of India's culinary culture.
